How to Play

Goal: Survive each stage, defeat monsters, and collect gold 💰 and diamonds 💎 to upgrade.

Controls (PC):

  • W A S D Move
  • Mouse Aim & Left Click Shoot
  • Shift or Space Dash
  • Q Ultimate
  • E Character's unique skill
  • Esc Pause

Controls (Mobile): Left stick to move, right stick to aim/shoot, skill buttons at the bottom.

Stage pickups: Shield (temporary invincibility), Speed Boost, Damage Boost – appear randomly 1-2 times per stage.

Maps: 3 large maps x 30 stages each. You must clear all 30 stages to unlock the next map (🔒 locked until then). Checkpoint every 5 stages. Boss every 10 stages.

Upgrades: After each stage win, choose 1 of 3 random boost cards (Damage / Lifesteal / Cooldown Reduction).

Survival Mode: Monsters keep spawning in waves, each wave larger, faster and tankier than the last. A Boss appears every 5 waves. The match only ends when you run out of health — your highest wave survived is recorded.

Fake Online (5vs5 Team Battle): A simulated online match — you and 4 AI allies fight 5 AI enemies. Bots move, aim and shoot on their own. The match ends when one whole team is wiped out, then a VICTORY/DEFEAT scoreboard shows every player's and bot's kill count.
